# Zenith Yoga & Wellness - Reencuéntrate

A calm, breathing-focused template for yoga studios, pilates centers, and holistic retreat planners.

## Design Philosophy
"Soft Focus & Clarity." Uses a palette of lavender, sage, and off-whites to reduce visual noise. Large white spaces and gentle animations foster a sense of peace.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Quicksand (Soft/Rounded) & Playfair Display (Serif/Elegant)
- **Primary Colors**: Lavender Cloud (#E6E6FA) and Sage Moss (#556B2F)
- **Interactive Elements**: Simulated "Breathe" meditation guide, Daily wellness quote generator, and a smooth-scroll class library.
