# Mar & Limón - Fresh Seafood Bar

A bright and modern template for seafood restaurants and cevicherías that focus on freshness and visual appeal.

## Design Philosophy
Oceanic and airy. Uses various shades of blue (the sea) mixed with vibrant lime/lemon greens (the acidity). High-key photography makes the raw seafood look appetizing and safe.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Modern Sans) & Capriola (Round Accent)
- **Primary Colors**: Oceanic Blue (#2980B9) and Zesty Lime (#27AE60)
- **Interactive Elements**: "Citrus Meter" for each dish, catch source map, and animated water ripples.
