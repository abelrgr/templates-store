# Voley Arena Bolivia - Pasión en la Arena

A vibrant, sun-soaked template designed for volleyball centers, beach clubs, and sports rental facilities.

## Design Philosophy
"Beach Vibes & High Jump." Combines warm sand tones (#FFC107) with dynamic ocean blues (#2196F3) to create an energetic, outdoor atmosphere.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Oswald (Bold/Athletic) & Montserrat (Readable)
- **Primary Colors**: Golden Sand (#FFC107) and Sky Blue (#2196F3)
- **Interactive Elements**: Hour-by-hour rental grid, Team scoreboard, and a "Jump Power" simulated training guide.
