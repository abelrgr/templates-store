# Artesania & Herencia - Bolivian Artisan Marketplace

A sophisticated e-commerce template dedicated to high-quality Bolivian handicrafts, from Potosí silver to Tarabuco textiles.

## Design Philosophy
Combines the vibrancy of Andean culture (Aguayo patterns) with a clean, museum-like digital layout.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Lora (Serif) & Inter (Sans)
- **Primary Colors**: Teracotta (#D35400) and Deep Indigo (#2D3436)
- **Interactive Elements**: Micro-animations for fabric textures and hover effects on intricate details.
