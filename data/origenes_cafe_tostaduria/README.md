# Orígenes Café & Tostaduría

A warm, sensory-focused template for specialty coffee shops that value the journey from bean to cup.

## Design Philosophy
Earthy tones, soft shadows, and high-quality photography focus. Uses serif typography to convey craftsmanship and heritage.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Crimson Pro (Serif) & Montserrat (Sans)
- **Primary Colors**: Deep Espresso (#4E342E) and Warm Crema (#D7CCC8)
- **Interactive Elements**: Brew method selector (V60, Chemex, Aeropress), flavor profile notes, and bean origin storytelling.
