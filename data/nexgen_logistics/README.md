# NexGen Logistics - Speed Guaranteed

A high-frequency logistics template optimized for express courier services and international document/parcel delivery.

## Design Philosophy
"Action and Energy." Inspired by global leaders like DHL, this template uses high-contrast Yellow (#FFCC00) and Red (#D40511) to trigger immediate recognition and a sense of urgency.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Roboto (Functional/Tech) & Passion One (Impact Headers)
- **Primary Colors**: Cyber Yellow (#FFCC00) and Post Red (#D40511)
- **Interactive Elements**: Shipment tracking simulator (X-tracking ID), Shipping cost estimator by weight/dimensions, and localized proximity map UI.
