# La Parrilla Boliviana - Steak & Fire

A high-end, dark-themed template for steakhouses and "parrilladas" that want to emphasize the quality of their meat and the mastery of the grill.

## Design Philosophy
Sophisticated and intense. Dark backgrounds with fire-inspired accents (red/orange). High-contrast product shots make the grilled textures pop.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Montserrat (Bold, Modern) & Playfair Display (Elegant Serif for headings)
- **Primary Colors**: Charcoal Black (#1C1C1C) and Ember Red (#C0392B)
- **Interactive Elements**: "Meat Doneness" slider (Rare, Medium, Well Done), Interactive Wine Library, and a charcoal ember background effect.
