# Vitalis Medical Center - Multi-Specialty Clinic

A professional, trust-inspiring template for general clinics, medical centers, and private practices.

## Features
- **Modern Healthcare Design:** Clean, clinical blue and white palette with professional typography.
- **Patient Appointment Booking:** Interactive scheduling interface powered by Alpine.js.
- **Medical Services Catalog:** Filterable list of specialties (General Medicine, Lab, Radiology, etc.).
- **Staff Directory:** Profiles of medical specialists with qualification details.
- **Emergency Modal:** Quick-access emergency information and contact triggers.
- **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for pharmacy lookups and mobile appointment management.
- **Dark Mode Support:** Easy on the eyes for late-night medical research.

## Use Cases
- Private Medical Clinics
- Diagnostic Centers
- Multi-specialty Hospitals
- Urgent Care Centers

## Technologies Used
- **Tailwind CSS:** Responsive utility-first framework.
- **Alpine.js:** State management for the appointment form, specialty filters, and modals.
- **Tabler Icons:** Standard medical and clinical iconography.
- **Google Fonts:** Inter (Sans-serif) and Plus Jakarta Sans.
