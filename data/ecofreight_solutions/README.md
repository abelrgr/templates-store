# EcoFreight Solutions - Shipping for the Future

A forward-thinking logistics template dedicated to sustainable distribution, carbon-neutral shipping, and circular economy packaging.

## Design Philosophy
"Harmony & Innovation." Uses a refreshed palette of Sea Green (#2E8B57) and Beige (#F5F5DC) to symbolize nature and clean technology. Focuses on clarity and planetary responsibility.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Montserrat (Balanced/Geometric) & Playfair Display (Sophisticated Accents)
- **Primary Colors**: Sea Green (#2E8B57) and Earthy Beige (#F5F5DC)
- **Interactive Elements**: Real-time CO2 savings estimator (based on shipping distance), modular sustainable packaging builder, and an interactive map of solar-powered logistics hubs.
