# Olympus Multisport Complex - Donde los Atletas se Forjan

A professional-grade template for large sports complexes, country clubs, and multi-discipline training centers.

## Design Philosophy
"Prestige & Performance." Uses a robust Navy Blue (#1B365D) to convey authority and trust, paired with International Orange (#FF4500) for dynamic action.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Montserrat (Geometric/Modern) & Archivo Black (Heavy Display)
- **Primary Colors**: Deep Navy (#1B365D) and Flame Orange (#FF4500)
- **Interactive Elements**: Discipline-based tabbed booking system, Animated "Peak Hours" chart, and a multi-step registration form for academies.
