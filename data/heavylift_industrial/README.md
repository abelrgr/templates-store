# HeavyLift Industrial - Moving the Unmovable

A specialized logistics template for high-tonnage transport, industrial machinery relocation, and large-scale warehousing.

## Design Philosophy
"Raw Strength & Reliability." Uses a bold industrial palette centered around Charcoal (#2D2D2D) and Safety Yellow (#FDB813) to evoke construction, power, and precision engineering.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Space Grotesk (Tech/Mechanical) & Barlow Condensed (Bold/Compact)
- **Primary Colors**: Anthracite Grey (#2D2D2D) and Signal Yellow (#FDB813)
- **Interactive Elements**: Load-bearing capacity calculator for heavy machinery, dynamic warehouse slot availability UI, and interactive safety procedure icons.
