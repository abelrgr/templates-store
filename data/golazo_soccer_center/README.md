# Golazo Soccer Center - Vive tu Pasión

The ultimate template for football/soccer pitch rentals, indoor futsal arenas, and sports academies.

## Design Philosophy
"Stadium Fever." Uses high-contrast deep greens and stadium blacks, with trophy gold accents. Designed to look like a high-end sports app.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Bebas Neue (Aggressive Sports Style) & Inter (Clean Content)
- **Primary Colors**: Turf Green (#28A745) and Pitch Black (#1A1A1A)
- **Interactive Elements**: Slot availability marquee, Team Manager dashboard skeleton, and pitch-type dynamic pricing.
