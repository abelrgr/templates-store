# Titan Auto Parts - Automotive Spares Platform

A high-performance template designed for shops selling mechanical parts, engine components, and aesthetics for vehicles.

## Design Philosophy
Heavy industrial aesthetic. Focuses on clarity, technical specifications, and reliability. High-contrast elements for readability in workshop environments.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Roboto (Condensed) & Orbitron (Display)
- **Primary Colors**: Dark Charcoal (#1A1A1B) and High-Vis Blue (#007BFF)
- **Interactive Elements**: Dynamic filters for Year/Make/Model, search suggestions, and stock availability indicators.
