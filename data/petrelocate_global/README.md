# PetRelocate International - Caring for Your Cuddly Travelers

A specialized boutique logistics template for international and domestic pet relocation, focusing on safety, legal compliance, and pet comfort.

## Design Philosophy
"Soft Trust & Safety." Uses a calming Teal (#008080) for authority and medical precision, paired with a warm Peach (#FFDAB9) to evoke friendliness and care.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Quicksand (Rounded/Friendly) & Fredoka One (Playful Headers)
- **Primary Colors**: Deep Teal (#008080) and Peach Puff (#FFDAB9)
- **Interactive Elements**: Pet travel requirements wizard (by country), step-by-step checklist with progress tracking, and a "Comfort Monitor" dashboard simulation.
