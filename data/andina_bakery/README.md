# Andina Bakery & Pastelería - El Aroma de la Altura

A warm, inviting, and highly visual template designed for artisanal bakeries and local pastry shops.

## Design Philosophy
"Farina & Grace." Uses soft beige and cream tones to resemble flour and dough. High-quality macro photography for textures.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Quicksand (Friendly/Rounded) & Cormorant Garamond (Classical/Artisanal)
- **Primary Colors**: Champagne (#F7E7CE) and Bark Brown (#A67C52)
- **Interactive Elements**: "Freshly Baked" live countdowns, Multi-layered subscription forms for morning delivery boxes, and an interactive "Tear the bread" hover effect.
