# El Palacio del Chicharrón - Tradición de Cochabamba

A festive and authentic template for traditional Bolivian pork restaurants, emphasizing the "Cochala" flavor and family gatherings.

## Design Philosophy
Warm, energetic, and rustic. Uses vibrant yellows (the golden crackling) and fiery reds. Large photography shows the texture of the pork and the steam of the mote.

## Technical Details
- **Fonts**: Arvo (Serif) & Lato (Sans)
- **Primary Colors**: Golden Crackling (#F1C40F) and Burnt Orange (#E67E22)
- **Interactive Elements**: "Paila" status (In-prep, cooking, serving), combo customizer (extra mote, llajua level), and delivery tracker.
