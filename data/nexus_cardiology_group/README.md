# Nexus Cardiology Group - Advanced Heart Care

A sophisticated, technology-focused template for cardiology centers, heart surgeons, and vascular specialists.

## Features
- **Precision Medical UI:** High-contrast design using deep crimson and navy tones with technical accents.
- **Heart Health Dashboard:** Interactive data visualization components (Pulse, BP ranges) for patient education.
- **Advanced Diagnostics Section:** Detailed overviews of EKG, Echocardiograms, and Stress Tests.
- **Referral Workflow:** Secure digital referral form for primary care physicians.
- **Specialist Profiles:** Focus on board certifications, research, and surgical success rates.
- **Risk Assessment Tool:** Simple Alpine.js-powered calculator for heart health awareness.
- **Dark/Light Mode:** Seamlessly switch between clinical light and high-tech dark themes.

## Use Cases
- Specialized Cardiology Clinics
- Heart Surgery Centers
- Vascular Diagnostic Hubs
- Cardiac Rehabilitation Facilities

## Technologies Used
- **Tailwind CSS:** Advanced grid layouts and pulse animation utilities.
- **Alpine.js:** State management for calculators, diagnostic tabs, and form validation.
- **Tabler Icons:** Specialized heart rate, medical monitoring, and vital sign icons.
- **Google Fonts:** Roboto (Technical) and Syne (Modern/Precision).
